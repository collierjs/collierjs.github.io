Jamie Collier

Cyber Security DPhil | Oxford, UK.

About | Research | Publications

I am a Cyber Security DPhil (PhD) Candidate based at the Department of Politics and International Relations and Centre for Doctoral Training in Cyber Security My research broadly examines the strategic and political aspects of cyber security. Within Oxford, I am a Research Affiliate with the Centre for Technology and Global Affairs where I coordinate cyber security activities and a Research Associate with the Changing Character of War Centre

In 2017, I was based based at the MIT as a Cyber Security Fulbright Scholar. I also have previous work experience with the NATO Cooperative Cyber Defence Centre of Excellence and PwC India.
